OUR STORY

Tradition Since 1955

Founded in Chiba in 1955, KIKUZONO has continued to craft traditional Japanese sweets with care and dedication.

Our signature warabimochi is loved for its soft texture, rich kinako flavor, and handcrafted quality.

We hope every visitor can experience a gentle moment of Japanese tradition through our sweets.
