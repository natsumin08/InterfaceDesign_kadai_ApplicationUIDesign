FAQ

Before Your Visit

Can I eat inside the shop?

KIKUZONO is mainly for takeout. Please enjoy our sweets after purchasing them.

Are the sweets available online?

Our sweets are available only at our Chiba shop. Please visit the store to purchase them.

Can I buy warabimochi as a souvenir?

Yes. Warabimochi is beautifully packaged and makes a special souvenir from Chiba.

How long does warabimochi last?

Warabimochi is best enjoyed within two days and should be kept refrigerated.
